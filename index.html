Valo's Among Us IRL

An unofficial system of playing a game similar to Among Us that uses real life as a medium.
QR Codes can be scanned, leading to tasks, which give codes you can enter into your local instance of the discord bot. The owner of the discord bot instance (set this via the bot properties file) creates game sessions with the "a.new" command, then starts them after everyone joins with the "a.start" command. The bot then delegates your tasks, and determines who the impostor(s) is/are. With an app this could be streamlined a lot more but this has been designed to not require a dedicated server, which such an app would require.


DISCLAIMER: There are a number of known ways to cheat, though some anti-cheat mechanisms are in place. Some of these are due to the nature of the medium. So you'll have to trust people to not immediately hide while completing tasks after scanning their qr codes. Of course, as this is played locally, rather than with online random players, and there is no real long term benefit or defecit for winning or losing, the likelyhood of individuals cheating is low.

Creating a properties.txt file for the bot

When running the bot, you'll need a properties.txt file. It should contain simple key=value formatted entries. The format for the properties is as such:

key=(bot key)
master=DiscordTagGoesHere#0000
